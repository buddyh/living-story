LOOP TITLE
Living Story Loop

PROMPT OR INSTRUCTIONS

Keep a daily, evidence-based picture of everything I am building, stored in [story dir]. On the first run, create what is missing: config.md with repo roots (default ~/repos unless I provide another path), discovered repositories, and optional non-git sources such as notes, task exports, or calendar exports; about-me.md with a short template for who I am, how I work, what drives me, and what counts as real progress; goals.md with goals, targets, dates, status, and notes. If about-me.md or goals.md are still empty templates, build the best story you can from activity alone and say plainly it will be thin until those files are filled in. Then once per [window, e.g. day]: inspect every git repository under the configured roots and collect git log --since, files changed, current branch, ahead/behind status, and last meaningful activity. Update [story dir]/projects/[repo].md with status (active / stalled / shipped), current branch, what moved, what is in progress, last shipped date, and STALE if no activity occurred in [stale=5] days. Read about-me.md, goals.md, the previous STORY.md, and the refreshed project files, then write a fresh STORY.md addressed to me in second person with: STORY (1-2 interpretive paragraphs, not a list), FOCUS (3-5 current attention points), DEADLINES (YYYY-MM-DD), OPEN THREADS (in-progress work and questions with days-open and STALE/NEEDS-REVIEW flags), and RECENT WINS. Verify before finishing: every recent win must cite real evidence such as a commit, release, closed task, deployed artifact, sent deliverable, or generated artifact path; every open thread from the previous story must be carried forward, moved to recent wins with proof, or marked STALE/NEEDS-REVIEW. Never silently drop a thread. Append the final STORY.md to [story dir]/history/[date].md and prepend one line to CHANGELOG.md saying what changed since last time. Stop when the snapshot is written and verification passes. Return STORY.md, project files touched, and the one-line change summary.
